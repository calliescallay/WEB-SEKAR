Shaney

Shani from JKT48 has been rocking her idol career for over nine years with the group. The singer, whose full name is Shani Indira Natio, just announced that she's graduating from the idol group that made her name. Shani was part of JKT48's third generation and served as the captain of the group for nine years since officially becoming a member in 2014.

Shani's Achievements

  • Winner of Sounsenkyo Twice: Shani has won the Sounsenkyo election twice, a prestigious event where fans vote for their favorite JKT48 members.

  • Center Position Three Times: Shani has held the center position, the prominent spot in JKT48 performances, three times, showcasing her exceptional talent and popularity.

  • Solo Song "Menanti": Shani has a solo song titled "Menanti," demonstrating her versatility as an artist.

  • Ranked in Sousenkyo Five Times: Shani has been ranked in the Sousenkyo election five times, reflecting her consistent popularity and support from fans.

  • Phenomenal Journey Once: Shani had a phenomenal journey in JKT48, making a significant impact during her time with the group.

  • Captain of JKT48 in the New Era: Shani successfully served as the Captain of JKT48 during the New Era, leading the group with dedication and professionalism.

Graduate

Singer Shani Indira Natio has just announced her graduation from the idol group JKT48. She conveyed this news during the JKT48 Summer Festival event held on Sunday (July 2, 2023).
